About the Project


In this project, we saw how PM2.5, PM10, NO, NO2, Nox, NH3, CO, SO2, O3, Benzene, Toulene and Xylene affect the Air Pollution and we took multiple cities to check the different trends, namely Mumbai, Delhi, Ahmedabad, Lucknow, Channai, Bengaluru and Hyderabad.

About the Creators


Being born in the 21st century, we hadn't ever seen any threat to humanity and when the covid-19 pandemic happened, all of us were shocked how quickly the nature can turn the tables on us. Now that we know that what the next pandemic is likely going to be, the climate change, we need to prepare ourselves for us and we decided to contribute in a small way by building a project aims to find the correlation of the different gases and the air pollution, and also based on the trends predict how clean will the air be in the coming years to raise awareness and help the people who are working on the actual solution.
